What is Gamer's Cove?


Gamer's Cove is gaming lounge located in Bhubaneswar, India. It was founded in 2016. The main goal of GC was to enable gamers experience the full capacity ofa video game and to encourage E-Sports in India. GC hosts many competitions - both online and offline to introduce gamers to a new reality. We house variety of systems that ranges from high end rigs to consoles such as Xbox, Playstation and Nintendo with sophisticated equipments. All the facilities are availaible at very affordable prices. We have partnered with several leading companies to bring you the ultimate experience.
